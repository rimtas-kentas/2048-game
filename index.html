How to Play 2048

Step 1: Basic Controls

Use your keyboard arrows to move the tiles:

  • ↑ - Move Up
  • ↓ - Move Down
  • ← - Move Left
  • → - Move Right

Step 2: Tile Movement

Tiles slide in the direction you move them. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Step 3: Scoring

Each time tiles merge, you earn points equal to the new tile's value. Try to reach the 2048 tile!
